MY CITY BALUSHAHI


Runnisaidpur is a town and a block in Sitamarhi district, Bihar,
India known for its delicious Balushahi sweet.
It is situated on the southern bank of the Lakhandei river.
National highway 77 passes through Runnisaidpur.
The regional language is Bajjika. Runnisaidpur has good quality of education.
There are good school like-DAV and DPS.
This is the border of Sitamarhi and Muzaffarpur district
